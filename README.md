# MogMatch
MogMatch is an anonymous video chat app where two strangers go head-to-head and get rated by AI in real time.
When a round starts, both webcams go live and the AI scans each face, assigning a score from 0 to 10. That score maps to a tier — Sub-3, Sub-5, LTN (Low-Tier Normie), MTN (Mid-Tier Normie), HTN (High-Tier Normie), Chad-Lite, or Chad. At the end of the scan, the app reveals who mogged who.
It's like Omegle, but instead of just chatting, you find out where you rank.
